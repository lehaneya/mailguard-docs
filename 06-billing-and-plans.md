## Overview

MailGuard offers flexible plans to fit businesses of any size. Start with a free trial, upgrade when you're ready, add domains as you grow.

## Plans at a Glance

### Standard Plan — $20/month

Best for: Small businesses, startups, single-domain monitoring

**Includes:**
- 1 domain monitored
- DNS checks: SPF, DKIM, DMARC, MX, BIMI, Blacklist, DANE, MTA-STS, TLS-RPT, PTR, Certificate Expiry, Subdomains
- Real-time email alerts
- Security score (0-100) and letter grade (A-F)
- Domain health dashboard
- Check history and reporting
- Public security page (shareable with stakeholders)
- Embeddable security badge

### Premium Plan — $30/month

Best for: Growing companies, security-conscious teams, enterprises

**Includes everything in Standard, plus:**
- AI-powered security analysis (Claude AI)
  - Plain-English summaries of email security
  - Risk assessments explaining business impact
  - Step-by-step action plans to fix issues
- DMARC aggregate report ingestion
  - Automatic report collection via unique RUA address
  - Manual XML/ZIP report upload
  - Report analytics and pass rate tracking
  - AI analysis of report data
- Lookalike domain monitoring (upcoming)
- Email header analyzer (upcoming)
- Compliance checker (upcoming)

### Additional Domains

Want to monitor more domains? Add them anytime for **$9.99/month each**.

**Example pricing:**
- Standard plan (1 domain): $20/month
- Standard + 2 additional domains: $20 + $19.98 = $39.98/month
- Premium plan (1 domain): $30/month
- Premium + 5 additional domains: $30 + $49.95 = $79.95/month

## Upgrading Your Plan

### From Trial to Standard

When your 14-day trial is ending (or anytime during):

1. Go to **Billing** page
2. You'll see the plans available
3. Click **"Start 14-day free trial"** on Standard
4. You'll be redirected to Stripe to enter payment info
5. Choose:
   - **Monthly billing** ($20/month, cancel anytime)
   - Or save your payment for after trial ends
6. Confirm subscription

Your trial period extends through the current 14 days, then transitions to paid.

### From Standard to Premium

Want AI and DMARC report features?

1. Go to **Billing** page
2. Scroll to "Upgrade to Premium"
3. Click **"Upgrade to Premium"**
4. You'll go to Stripe checkout
5. Confirm upgrade

**Proration:** If you're mid-month on Standard, you'll only pay the difference for the remaining days.

### From Trial to Premium

If you want to skip Standard and go straight to Premium:

1. Go to **Billing** page
2. Under the plan options, click **"Start 14-day free trial"** on Premium
3. Enter payment info
4. You get Premium features for the trial period, then pay $30/month

---

## Downgrading Your Plan

Downgrade from Premium to Standard anytime. No penalty.

1. Go to **Billing** page
2. Click **"Manage Subscription"**
3. Under "Plan," select **Standard**
4. Confirm downgrade
5. You lose Premium features (AI, DMARC reports) immediately, but AI analyses are archived
6. Monthly charge drops to $20/month

**Note:** If you have more than 1 domain, you'll be billed for additional domain slots, even on Standard plan.

## Managing Subscriptions (Stripe Portal)

MailGuard uses Stripe to manage payments. You can manage everything from the Stripe portal:

### Accessing Stripe Portal

1. Go to **Billing** page in MailGuard
2. Click **"Manage Subscription"**
3. You're taken to Stripe portal
4. Log in with your email (same as MailGuard account)

### In Stripe Portal You Can

- **Update payment method** (add new credit card, update expiration, etc.)
- **Change billing email** (where invoices are sent)
- **View invoice history** (all past charges)
- **Cancel subscription** (stop billing immediately or at end of period)
- **Download invoices** (for accounting/expense reports)

## Adding Additional Domains

You need more domains to monitor? Add them anytime, even mid-month.

### How to Add a Domain Slot

1. Go to **Billing** page
2. Look for "Additional Domain Slot" section
3. Click **"Purchase additional domain slot"** (or "Add domain slot")
4. You go to Stripe checkout
5. Confirm purchase: $9.99/month
6. You can immediately add a new domain in MailGuard

### Billing for Additional Domains

- **Monthly charge:** $9.99 per additional domain
- **Prorated:** If purchased mid-month, you pay a prorated amount
- **Cancel anytime:** Stop paying when you remove the domain

### Removing a Domain

1. Go to the domain in MailGuard
2. Click **"Delete"** (top right)
3. Confirm deletion
4. Domain is removed; you can remove the domain slot from Stripe to stop paying

## Cancellation Policy

### Canceling Your Subscription

You can cancel anytime, no questions asked.

1. Go to **Billing** page
2. Click **"Manage Subscription"**
3. In Stripe portal, go to **Subscriptions**
4. Click **"Cancel Subscription"**
5. Choose:
   - **Cancel immediately** (stop charging now; lose access)
   - **Cancel at end of billing period** (keep access until next billing date)

### After Cancellation

- **Access:** You lose access to MailGuard immediately (or at period end)
- **Data:** Your domain data and alert history are deleted after 30 days
- **Reactivation:** You can resubscribe anytime; you'll start fresh

### Trial Cancellation

If you cancel during the 14-day trial, no charges. Your account is simply deleted.

## Failed Payments

If a payment fails (expired card, insufficient funds, etc.):

1. **You'll receive an email** from Stripe notifying you
2. **Access continues** for 3 days while you fix it
3. **Update your payment method** in Stripe portal
4. **Automatic retry** happens within 24 hours
5. **If still fails after 3 days,** access is suspended until payment is fixed

### Preventing Failed Payments

- Update expiring credit cards proactively
- Keep billing email current (so you see notifications)
- Ensure sufficient funds

## Refund Policy

### Refunds

MailGuard offers **pro-rata refunds** if you cancel mid-month.

**Example:**
- Charged $20 on April 1 (Standard plan)
- Cancel on April 15 (after 15 days)
- Refund: ~$10 (refund for unused days)

**Process:**
1. Cancel subscription in Stripe
2. Refund is processed within 5-10 business days
3. Appears back on your credit card

### Non-Refundable Items

- Additional domain slots (once charged, no refund)
- Partial months (refunded pro-rata, not full refunds)

## Billing FAQ

### Q: Can I change my billing cycle (monthly to annual)?

**A:** Currently, MailGuard only offers monthly billing. Annual discounts are on the roadmap. Contact support to request.

---

### Q: What happens if I reach my domain limit?

**A:** If you have 3 domain slots (1 from plan + 2 additional) and try to add a 4th:

1. You'll get a message saying you've reached your limit
2. Go to Billing and purchase another domain slot ($9.99/month)
3. Come back and add the domain

---

### Q: Can I change my plan mid-month?

**A:** Yes. If you upgrade/downgrade:

- **Upgrade:** You pay the difference for the remaining days at the higher rate
- **Downgrade:** You receive a pro-rata credit for remaining days

**Example:** It's April 15. You're on Standard ($20/month). You upgrade to Premium ($30/month). You pay ~$5 for the remaining 15 days of April.

---

### Q: Can I have multiple Stripe accounts for different teams?

**A:** Each MailGuard account is tied to one Stripe customer. If you need separate billing for different teams, you can:

1. Create separate MailGuard accounts (different email addresses)
2. Each will have its own Stripe subscription
3. Invoices will be separate
